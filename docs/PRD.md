# AssamPrep - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Project Overview

AssamPrep is a modern online learning platform dedicated to Assam Government competitive examinations. The first version focuses on high-quality online mock tests, with future expansion into notes, PYQs, current affairs, AI assistance, and premium learning resources.

---

## 2. Goals

- Provide realistic mock tests

- Help students track performance

- Create a fast, mobile-friendly platform

- Support thousands of concurrent users

- Build a scalable product for future growth

---

## 3. Target Exams

- APSC CCE

- ADRE

- Assam Police

- Assam TET

- Grade III

- Grade IV

- Engineering Recruitment

- Other Assam Government Exams

---
